Dry-Run Workshop Results

Overall Feedback from our Dry-Run Participants

They think that time management is important because it helps me know what to do and organizes it to mitigate the stress and finish the tasks efficiently. Most of them liked our workshop while one was not sure about it. Our participants wanted us to improve on our workshop and provided us with possible improvements. A presentation and visuals will make it better. Giving the presenter more time to study so this will reduce the dead air.

Usability Responses

We had 12 questions in total and provided below are their responses either summarized or in graph form.

What is time management to you?

Time management is about doing your task perfectly at a short amount of time.

Is time management useful in your day to day life? Why?

Yes, because it helps me know what to do and organizes it to mitigate the stress and finish the tasks efficiently.

Do you want a game like activity in our workshop?

Do you want to see these questions in our workshop?

How can we do more tasks in less time?

All of our participants wanted to see this question in our workshop.

All of our participants wanted to see this question in our workshop.
