About our Workshop

What is our workshop all about?

Our workshop is all about Time Management.

What will we get from this workshop?

After this workshop we will provide you with ways to improve your time management skills.

What will the workshop contain?

The workshop will contain a short ice-breaker/game at the start of the workshop. Next, the participants will share their ways of managing their time and talk with each other to improve each other's way of managing. After that, the host will give tips to further improve their skills.

Where and when is the workshop?

Our workshop will be hosted on Zoom Rooms and the date is to be decided.

Where can we sign up?

If you are an APEC Student please fill in your APEC Gmail.
